About Us

The Science and Engineering Business Association (SEBA) is an undergraduate organization that unites students majoring in technical fields by introducing them to business fields such as finance and management. SEBA focuses on professional development, as well as education supplement and networking.

The objective is to prepare students for careers in business and management. By providing networking opportunities, establishing mentoring relationships, and offering various events and forums, SEBA hopes to broaden students' understanding of business and its complexities.

Our History

The Science and Engineering Business Association first started in the Spring of 2011. Seeing the need for a business club accessible to engineering students, the founding members decided to explore what it would take to fill this gap. Working with an MBA student, the officers pieced together an organization that would provide services to its members while also allowing for networking opportunities.
